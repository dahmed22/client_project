Networking Strategies for Success

Networking is a crucial skill for career development, allowing you to connect with professionals, gain insights, and uncover opportunities. Whether you’re just starting to build your network or looking to expand it, these resources are here to help you every step of the way.

UMSI students are in high demand, and a clear, well-organized resume will help you stand out among applicants. On this page, you'll find practical tips, templates, and guides—all tailored to the needs of School of Information students and aligned with employer expectations.

Need guidance? You can always meet with a CDO Career Coach to plan a networking approach.

Networking Made Easy: Steps to Success

  1. Attend CDO Coffee Chats and Company Info Sessions: The CDO hosts events all throughout the year to help you connect with industry professionals.
  2. Reach out to Alumni and Industry Professionals: Alumni are a valuable resource for networking and can provide insights into their career paths.
  3. Use LinkedIn: LinkedIn is not only great for job searching, but allows you to reach out to people that work in companies your interested in.
  4. Tailor Your Approach: Customize your outreach for every connection you pursue.
  5. Stay Connected: Follow up with new contacts, share relevant articles, and engage with their content.

Networking FAQ

  • Should I prepare a pitch?
    Yes! Preparing a pitch can be the quickest way to provide information about you and your interests and a great conversation starter. The CDO provides resources on preparing a pitch, check them out!
  • Is it too late to network?
    It’s never too late to network! Whether you’re a first-year student or about to graduate, building and maintaining a network is a lifelong skill that can open doors at any stage of your career.
  • What should I do if I don't receive a response?
    Follow up politely! Sometimes emails get lost or overlooked. A gentle reminder can help bring your message back to the recipient's attention.
  • How do I maintain a connection?
    Update your LinkedIn profile regularly, share relevant articles or insights, and engage with your connections' posts to keep the relationship alive.

CDO Fun Fact: What is LAMP?

LAMP stands for List, Alumni, Motivation, and Posting. It is a strategic approach to networking that helps you build meaningful relationships with professionals in your field. By actively listening, asking thoughtful questions, making genuine connections, and planning follow-ups, you can create a strong network that supports your career growth.
