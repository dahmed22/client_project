What can you do with Information?

A career in Information has many pathways. Whether its archiving, data management, user experience design, or information policy, the possibilities are vast and varied. Explore different roles, industries, and career trajectories to find what aligns best with your skills and passions.

Start your pathway by understanding the high demand skills that comes with your degree. Do you envision yourself working on Figma to tackle a design problem? Or using Python to understand how your clients interact with data? What problems do you want to solve?

Need guidance? You can always meet with a CDO Career Coach to talk about your career goals.

Career Exploration Made Easy: Steps to Success

  1. Take a Career Assessment The University Career Center provides career assessments for students exploring career options.
  2. Network with Alumni and Industry Professionals: The best way to find out if a career path is right for you is to connect with those who are already working in the field.
  3. Attend Workshops and Events: Speak to recruiters and learn about how your skills align with industry needs.
  4. Alumni Connections: Leverage UMSI's alumni network to gain insights and advice from those who have successfully navigated similar paths.
  5. UMSI Career Link: Access job postings, internships, and career resources tailored for UMSI students.

Career Exploration FAQ

  • Do I have enough time to explore career paths?
    Yes! UMSI offers a variety of resources and support to help you explore different career options at your own pace. However, it's recommended that you finalize your program pathway by the end of your first semester (Graduate students).
  • How do I find out about the different career paths available to me?
    Explore UMSI's resources, attend workshops, and connect with alumni to learn about various career trajectories.
  • Can my coursework help me with career exploration?
    Absolutely! UMSI coursework and projects provide valuable skills and experiences that can inform your career choices.
  • Can I change career paths coming from a non-Information background?
    Absolutely! Many UMSI students come from diverse backgrounds and successfully transition into information-related careers. Leverage your unique experiences and skills to explore new opportunities.
